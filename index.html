Our Services

COMBINED HEALTH & SAFETY/ FIRE RISK ASSESSMENTS

The objective of a Fire Risk Assessment is to produce a flexible, dynamic system that establishes and maintains fire safety in a premises and its vicinity. Every premises generates hazards and risks, and to ensure the safety of all persons who visit the premises. We are now able to offer Fire Risk Assessments to PAS 79 standards as recognised by the Institute of Fire Safety Managers (IFSM).

FIRE ALARM SYSTEMS

The purpose of a fire alarm system is to alert building occupants in the early stages of a fire condition. It is essential this equipment is correctly maintained and regularly tested in accordance with BS 5839. We are able to maintain and repair all types of commercial and domestic fire alarm systems. We also offer a 24/7 out of hours call out service.

EMERGENCY LIGHTING

Emergency lighting is a vital part of your building. This is designed to illuminate in the event of a power failure to aid safe escape from the building. Emergency lighting needs to comply to BS 5266 and requires periodic testing. We are able to offer regular testing and repairs of all types of emergency lighting.
